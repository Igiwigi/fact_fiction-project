Terrific peals of thunder, preceded by
vivid flashes of weird lightning, helped to increase the depression of spirits caused by the gloom, and
gave rise to a feeling that the worst had not yet been experienced. Judging from appearances at this
time, one would have concluded that there was going to take place a heavy downpour of rain. As a
matter of fact, however, none fell. The grooves of the tramway rails filled rapidly with the dusty preci-
pitation, and it was simply impossible to keep them clear. As a consequence, the cars ran off repeatedly,
and ultimately the service had to be stopped sometime before the usual hour.
