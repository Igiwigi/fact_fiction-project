Note the discrepancy in the titles given for von Mises’ book; apparently, the translator made
an error here. In fact, von Neumann also errs in this passage, as the ‘ihre’ is an interpolation. Nevertheless, the meaning of the passage is clear: in 1932, von Neumann interpreted probability
in a frequentist manner. Evidence of this occurs throughout MFQM, in fact.
