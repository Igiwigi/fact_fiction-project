Each emphasized what the other could do, insisting they
were envious of their prowess and inspired to study harder. Neither could be accused of ‘being
their own Trumpeter’, allowing Lalande to generously perform that task for them himself. Caroline’s paper is an announcement of the existence of a previously unknown comet, so
simply within those terms it is as factually accurate today as it was when it was written. The
comet exists and had not been spotted before (unlike some of Caroline’s later comet discoveries). Her paper makes no attempt to analyse the comet's orbit, or make predictions about its future
position or date of return.
